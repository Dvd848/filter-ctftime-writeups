Filter your CTFTime Writeups feed

The CTFTime Writeups RSS feed allows you to get notified when a new writeup is added to CTFTime. That happens a lot.

This mini-service offers you the ability to filter the writeups feed, so that you only see writeups for CTFs that you're interested in.
